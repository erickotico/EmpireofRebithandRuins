About the Studio

Who We Are

We are Myth Forge, an independent development studio dedicated to creating immersive experiences. Founded with a passion for epic storytelling and challenging gameplay, we specialize in detailed worldbuilding and innovative mechanics.

Our Mission

Create games that expand the horizons of imagination, offering players memorable stories and living worlds to shape their own adventures.

Our Vision

Become a reference in the gaming industry, known for artistic quality, engaging narratives, unique mechanics, and a dedicated community around our projects.
